Specialities

We are specialists in different modalities. Count on us for your sessions and events.



Portrait

Photograph of a person, in which the face and its expression is predominant. The intent is to display the likeness, personality, and even the mood of the person.

Made by Alejandro Sedano Sanchez

Landscape

Landscape photographs typically capture the presence of nature but can also focus on man-made features or disturbances of landscapes.

Made by Louis Rodriguez Machado

Macro

Macro photography is about photographing objects that are very close to the lens, the film or the sensor. Using the classic definition, in macro photography the image shown on the film plane (or sensor) is nearly as big as the object photographed.

Made by Amtonio Morales Redondo

Architecture

Architectural photography is the photographing of buildings and similar structures that are both aesthetically pleasing and accurate representations of their subjects. Architectural photographers are usually skilled in the use of specialized techniques and cameras.

Made by Carlos Triguero Pardo
